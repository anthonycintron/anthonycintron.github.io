Time USA is an award-winning, branded content studio. Time is driving the conversations that shape tomorrow to help build a better future. Their trust, scale and access to the most influential and impactful people and ideas can offer consumers at every age ways to learn, nurture and grow.


Problem

Time is always innovating on new product solutions so that they can increase their market share in the media industry. The executive team hired me to work closely with their CTO, VP, Product and Studio team on a new product for the Netflix-type of consumer. I had 10 days to conduct market and customer research, ideate a competitive user experience, and deliver an interactive prototype for usability testing.


Solution

Our goal was to explore new opportunities to provide access to genius cuts and singular home premium, trusted content for personal growth, education and empowerment.

Market Research

I ran a competitive analysis against MasterClass, and studied their customer base's thoughts and feelings about their product experience. Through this market research, I was able to identify the negative experiences within the MasterClass application. My market research enabled us to better understand which areas within a course platform can be improved. As a result of our efforts, the competitive analysis helped us identify ideas on how to differentiate our product.


Prototype Demo

Interactive prototype demonstrating how Time Academy works.

Ideation

Ideated key ideas that would differentiate the Time Academy product against all products in the market. Time executive team was able to review sketches, low-fidelity wire-frames, and storyboards. The visual designs paved the way to building clarity on what the end product would achieve and how users could onboard, engage with celebrity instructors, and participate in community-based activities.


Prototyping

Directed UX design team on creating an interactive prototype for web and mobile experiences. Executive members were able to share interactive prototypes and demo videos with key stakeholders. The interactive prototype explored all key experiences within the application.
