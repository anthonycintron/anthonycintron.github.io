Problem

Time is innovating on new product solutions so that they may increase their market share in the media industry. The executive team hired me to work closely with their product and development team on a new product for the Netflix consumer. I had 10 days to conduct market and customer research, ideate a user experience that is competitive and deliver an interactive prototype for usability testing.

Objective

Explore new opportunities to provide access to genius cuts and singular home premium, trusted content for personal growth, education and empowerment.

Solution

  • Defined product design and strategy to match business objectives
  • Defined and designed customer journey and user personas
  • Conducted research on market demand
  • Executed user feedback and competitive landscape
  • Designed and built low-fidelity wire-frames
  • Directed and manage high-fidelity design and prototype process
