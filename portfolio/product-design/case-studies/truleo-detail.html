Problem

Truleo was pre-selling their product and services, and they needed to deliver a product solution for their early adopters within 6 months. The team had challenges adapting to a product-centered organization and needed help with lifting their product development initiatives due to being an engineering and sales-focused organization. Furthermore, the team lacked the capabilities to deliver an efficient customer development process for validating product value.

Objective

Execute a highly effective product design process which delivers a feasible prototype and usable beta solution.

Solution

Design Process

Facilitated daily design thinking sessions with Chief Product Officer, VP of Product and engineering team. I initially focused on the users-workflow beginning from onboarding and to the end when a Chief of Police needed to build a report on risky officer behavior. After mapping the users' journey, we were able to go through a divergent and convergent thinking process which is a technique that allowed us to iterate through designs while ensuring a high quality user experience.

Prototype

We built our first prototype using a spreadsheet and Miro (a whiteboarding tool). While the engineering team compiled real-time data, we applied customer data to a report in a PDF format. This made it easy to deliver an immediate solution to users for testing usability and our target user's sentiment. As we were iterating through each prototype build, we were refining our customer development program. Our customer development initiative brought in a ton of valuable feedback which informed the original release candidate for pre-sold customers.

Beta Release

After several iterations, we were able to deliver a release candidate to four California police departments. Within six months, we were able to achieve a sales pipeline, and convert customer development participants to early adopter customers. At this point, Truleo started generating revenue and has validated their minimal viable product.
